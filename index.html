The word 'slut' emits thousand feelings at once and unfortunately used as a tool to suppress the women in every sense. Everywhere around I have witnessed these words thrown on the women by the so called men. It takes just one second to call a woman a slut. Hence, a second is enough to outrage the modesty of a woman. Being a slut is not just a confinement of words, but a mirror of feelings, emotions to the world about how a girl feels when she faces the cruel world. It is a story from her eyes, the eyes which holds the unbearable pain, the pain which last until she dies.

Being a slut will not just be confined to book but will be taken ahead as a cause to open some vicious eyes and to heal up some wounds of beloved women. Being a slut is a vision to make the world a better place for women.
